Pull Down

    In the process of making this website, I encountered many problems. First of all, I only considered the format of the web template when I started making this netizen page but ignored the applicability of the template on the mobile phone. This problem caused me to complete the design. After the web page template needs to be redesigned for the mobile phone, because I ignored this problem, I need to spend more time and energy to complete it. It also makes me understand that I need to make sufficient preparations in advance when designing a website. I encountered many difficulties in the process. I used chapGPT or Google to search for answers to the problems, but when they were used in practice, the expected results could not be achieved.

    Design and Layout: Creating an attractive and pleasing-looking design for my website can be a challenge. In order to overcome this problem, I first researched other personal websites and collected inspiration for my own website. I prefer landscapes, so most of the pictures and designs are related to landscapes. After sorting and combining the collected inspirations, I finally get a relatively Satisfactory effect.

    Content Creation: It can be challenging to develop high-quality content that accurately represents my skills and achievements and showcases my personal abilities. It starts with identifying my unique selling points, including my work history, hobbies, and accomplishments, and crafting a compelling story that highlights my strengths. But the simple and elegant design is my personal style. By referring to other excellent personal websites and searching for some content creation tutorials, through continuous modification and improvement, I finally achieved the desired effect.

    Technical skills: Building a website requires technical skills such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am not familiar with or understand many professional codes, which poses a new challenge for me. By constantly replaying the teacher's lectures, searching professional websites, and even consulting some experienced people in this field, continuous exploration and research, finally achieve the desired effect.

    Network optimization: After the website is roughly completed, it may be a new challenge to optimize it, which is a big challenge for my personal aesthetics and personal skills, including font size, color, and the placement and order of pictures , need to be constantly modified, I also need to optimize the picture and shorten the page loading time, in order to overcome this challenge, I studied a lot of professional websites, modify the code to achieve the effect I want.
